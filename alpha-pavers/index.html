About Us

Tampa Bay's Brick Paver Specialists

Alpha Pavers is a locally owned paver company serving Clearwater and the greater Tampa Bay area. With 14 years of experience, we specialize in everything brick and stone — from custom paver driveways, patios, and pool decks to walkways and sidewalks. We also clean, seal, and restore existing pavers to bring them back to life. Every project is designed and installed for lasting beauty and durability in the Florida climate, and every job starts with a free estimate.
