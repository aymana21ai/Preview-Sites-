About Us

Healthy, Clean Water for Tampa Bay

Advanced Water is a locally owned, licensed water treatment company based in New Port Richey and serving the greater Tampa Bay area. We handle city water and well water alike, offering new and reconditioned softeners, whole-house filtration, and reverse osmosis drinking systems — backed by free water testing, free service calls, and free inspections. Our mission is simple: healthy, clean, quality water for every home we serve.
